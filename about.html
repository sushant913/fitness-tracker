About Fitness Tracker

Our Mission

The Fitness Tracker web app was created to provide a simple, intuitive, and effective tool for individuals to monitor their physical activity and progress. In an age where staying fit is crucial, having a reliable way to track your workouts can be a significant motivator.

Our goal is to empower you by offering a personalized overview of your fitness journey, helping you identify patterns, celebrate milestones, and stay consistent with your training goals.

How to Use This App

  • Home: Get an overview of the app and quick links to key features.
  • Workout Logger: This is where the magic happens! Fill in the exercise name, sets, reps, and optionally weight. Click "Add Workout" to save your entry. All your entries will appear in the table below. You can remove past entries using the "Remove" button.
  • Progress Tracker: See a summary of all your logged workouts. This page gives you a snapshot of your activity and details on individual workout sessions.
  • About: You're here! Learn about the app's purpose and how it was built.
  • Contact: Have questions or feedback? Use the form to send us a message.

Remember, your workout data is saved directly in your browser using `localStorage`. This means your data will persist even if you close and reopen the browser, but it's specific to the browser and device you're using.
